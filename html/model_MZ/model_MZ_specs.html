Model MZ Technical Specifications

Platform & Chassis

The Model MZ is built on a dedicated electric vehicle platform designed to optimize weight distribution, cabin space, and driving stability. The flat battery layout lowers the center of gravity, improving handling and ride comfort across different driving conditions.

Suspension & Braking

  • Front Suspension: MacPherson Strut
  • Rear Suspension: Multi-link independent setup
  • Brakes: Ventilated disc brakes (front & rear)
  • Regenerative braking with adjustable intensity

Driving Modes

Select a driving mode to see how it affects the vehicle.

Eco Mode

Designed for maximum efficiency:

  • Throttle response softened
  • Regenerative braking increased
  • Power output reduced to 220 HP
  • 0-100 km/h: 6.2 s

Comfort Mode

Balanced for everyday driving:

  • Standard throttle response
  • Regenerative braking moderate
  • Power output 280 HP
  • 0-100 km/h: 5.5 s

Sport Mode

Optimized for dynamic performance:

  • Throttle response sharpened
  • Steering assistance reduced
  • Power output increased to 330 HP
  • 0-100 km/h: 4.8 s

Snow Mode

Optimized for low-traction conditions:

  • Torque output limited
  • Traction control enhanced
  • Power output 240 HP
  • 0-100 km/h: 6.5 s
