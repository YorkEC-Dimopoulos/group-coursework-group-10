Winged Model V

A road‑legal vision of the sky. Model V – Winged is a celebration of open‑air freedom, sculpted for those who refuse to be grounded. Every line, every surface, every detail is drawn to capture the feeling of flight at speed.

DESIGN IN MOTION

Sculpted by the wind

The silhouette of Model V is defined by a single gesture: a sweeping wing‑like arc that frames the cockpit and flows into the rear. Air is not just managed – it is choreographed, guided over the body to create downforce, stability and a visual signature that is instantly recognizable, day or night.

OPEN‑SKY EXPERIENCE

A cockpit above the clouds

Inside, the world falls away. The open cockpit places the driver at the center of a cinematic horizon, with every control milled, stitched and crafted to feel timeless. Premium leathers, brushed metal, and ambient lighting create a sanctuary that balances raw performance with quiet, focused calm.

ELECTRIC HEART

Fly‑35: instant elevation

At the core of Model V lies the Fly‑35 electric power unit – engineered for explosive, seamless acceleration. Instant torque launches the car with a force that feels more like takeoff than departure. Precision torque vectoring keeps every movement controlled, turning power into pure confidence.

CARBON SCULPTURE

Lightness with purpose

The body of Model V is crafted from advanced carbon composites, each panel shaped to serve both beauty and function. Integrated aero channels, floating fins and a tapered rear profile reduce drag while enhancing stability, allowing the car to slice through the air with minimal resistance and maximum grace.

RARE BY DESIGN

A limited vision of the sky

Model V – Winged is not built for everyone. Each example is individually specified, hand‑finished and assembled with obsessive attention to detail. It is a statement of intent: a machine for those who see the road not as a boundary, but as a runway.
