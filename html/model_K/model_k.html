The Model K

Combination of power and mileage.

Design

Witness the collision of art and aerodynamics, with this extremely stylish SUV, colored in Polar White featuring an Obsidian Black roof. From the unique LED Matrix, to the 3D logo, this SUV stands out from the competition!

Performance

Powered by dual carbon-sleeved motors, the Model K delivers an outstanding 520 Horsepower.
It can go from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.9s, thanks to our proprietary 2-speed automatic transmission.
AWD: Intelligent Torque vectoring keeps you glued to the tarmak, rain or shine.

Range

You no longer need to worry about an electric car's range with the Model K SUV! The 130 kWh Semi-Solid State Battery can achieve an amazing 815 km with one charge*.
Charging time: With our high-voltage, 900V charger, you can go from 10% to 80% in just 18 minutes.

Interior

Step into the luxury Velvet Red interior of this car, featuring premium leather, and be amazed. With a Digital instrument screen behind the steering wheel, featuring an analog speedometer, and a Heads-Up Display (HUD), it nicely blends technology with an old-school style. The rear of the car features a mini fridge for drinks and snacks for long journeys and selective passengers alike! Champagne or soda? The choice is yours!
