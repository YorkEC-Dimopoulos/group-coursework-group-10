Design

Model L is everything people exempt from modern electric car design. A sleek futuristic design that doesn't draw too much attention, but it's still elegant and modern.

Luxury interior

The interior of Model L is a blend of modern and classic. The interior is a classic wooden design that features a futuristic control panel and automatic shift. This attracts lovers of the old car and new cars. The back of a car has three seats which can be helpful for transporting multiple people.

Range

With The Model L you don't need to worry about range. The 130 kWh Semi-Solid State Battery can achieve up to 815 km one one charge*.
Charging time: With our high-voltage, 900V charger, you can go from 10% to 80% in just 18 minutes.

Performance

The Model L is powered by Carbon-sleeved motors and delivers 520 Horsepower. It can go from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.9s, thanks to our proprietary 2-speed automatic transmission.
