MODEL V

Model Winged

Model V — Winged is a road‑legal vision shaped by the language of flight. Every surface and every contour is sculpted to evoke the sensation of rising above the world rather than simply moving through it. The open‑sky cockpit places the driver at the center of a cinematic horizon, while the sweeping wing‑arc silhouette defines its unmistakable identity — a fusion of elegance, precision, and raw elevation. Powered by the Fly‑35 electric heart, Model V delivers instant, seamless thrust that feels closer to takeoff than acceleration. Hand‑finished and produced in extremely limited numbers, it is crafted for those who refuse to be grounded — a machine for drivers who see the road not as a boundary, but as a runway.

MODEL K

MODEL K

Model K — Kinetic is a road-legal statement forged from motion itself. Every line and surface is engineered to express controlled force and forward intent, emphasizing momentum over ornament. The driver-focused cockpit wraps tightly around its core, creating an immersive sense of connection between human and machine, while the bold, muscular silhouette establishes a presence that feels grounded, deliberate, and unmistakably powerful. Driven by the K-Pulse electric system, Model K delivers relentless, linear acceleration — not explosive, but commanding, like an unstoppable surge. Hand-assembled and produced in strictly limited numbers, it is built for those who value precision, dominance, and purpose — a machine for drivers who see the road not as a stage, but as a proving ground.

MODEL L

MODEL L

The Model L leans into a sleek, streamlined look, giving it a lighter, more agile presence compared to bulkier electric SUVs. It’s powered by a single high‑efficiency motor or an optional dual‑motor upgrade, delivering smooth acceleration and quiet, confident driving. Inside, the Model L focuses on comfort: soft ambient lighting, a clean dashboard layout, and intuitive controls that keep everything simple. Its battery offers a solid everyday range, and fast‑charging support makes it easy to keep moving. Overall, the Model L feels like a practical, stylish EV built for drivers who want efficiency without sacrificing modern design.

MODEL MZ

MODEL MZ

The Model MZ comes across as a confident, modern electric SUV with a clean silhouette and a slightly sporty edge. Its dual‑motor setup gives it quick, effortless acceleration, while the all‑wheel‑drive system keeps it stable on rough roads or in bad weather. The cabin focuses on simplicity: a wide digital display, smart connectivity, and comfortable seating that feels premium without being flashy. Its battery offers a dependable long‑range capability, and fast‑charging support means you can top up quickly during short stops. Smooth, quiet, and efficient, the Model MZ is built for drivers who want practicality wrapped in a futuristic, stylish package.
